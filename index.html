Fellowship Program

It's a social initiative from DIPS Pvt limited for those who don't want their disability or financial position to play as a hindrance on their pathway of higher education. DIPS provides scholarship in way of fee waiver for those who are Differently Abled Person (MRFDAP) or economically weaker segment (EWS) or Women from rural area (WRA). Scholarship can go upto 100% fee waiver depending on the candidate's profile who wish to avail the scholarship. There is limited seat in each category. Students of Mathematics and Statistics from all over India who are willing to pursue higher education or research in Mathematics and statistics can apply for the fellowship in suitable category. This fellowship program support such student in preparation for IIT JAM (MATH & STAT) and CSIR NET (Math).
